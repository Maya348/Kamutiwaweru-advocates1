Our Legal Services

Kamutiwaweru & Co. Advocates offers a wide range of high-quality legal services, delivered by experienced and specialized professionals. Our practice spans across multiple sectors, offering tailored solutions for businesses and individuals alike.

Corporate Law

We specialize in providing expert legal counsel to corporations, from formation to dissolution. Our services include drafting and reviewing contracts, mergers and acquisitions, shareholder agreements, and corporate governance. We ensure businesses comply with all local regulations and help them navigate through complex corporate structures.

Family Law

Our family law experts assist clients in navigating difficult personal matters, including divorce, child custody, spousal support, and property settlements. We provide sensitive, discreet, and practical legal support, ensuring that our clients' best interests are always protected during emotionally charged situations.

Real Estate & Property Law

We offer comprehensive legal services in property acquisition, sale, leasing, and land disputes. Our team provides legal guidance on zoning, planning, and real estate regulations. Whether you're purchasing a home or managing commercial property, we ensure every transaction is legally sound and meets all statutory requirements.

Litigation & Dispute Resolution

Our litigation department handles both civil and commercial cases, representing clients in court. We specialize in alternative dispute resolution methods such as arbitration and mediation to help resolve conflicts efficiently. Our approach is focused on achieving favorable outcomes while minimizing disruption to our clients' operations.

Intellectual Property

We offer comprehensive intellectual property services, including patent protection, trademark registration, copyright enforcement, and licensing agreements. Our firm helps innovators protect their creations and intellectual assets, ensuring that their ideas and products are legally safeguarded in a global marketplace.

Employment Law

Our employment law team advises both employers and employees on workplace rights and responsibilities. We assist with issues such as wrongful termination, employee contracts, non-compete clauses, workplace discrimination, and harassment claims. We strive to create fair and legally compliant working environments.

Banking & Finance Law

We provide legal services in banking and financial transactions, including loan agreements, debt recovery, insolvency, and regulatory compliance. Our team is skilled in structuring financing deals, ensuring that financial institutions and borrowers alike comply with applicable laws and minimize risk in their dealings.

Taxation Law

Our tax law services include advising individuals and businesses on tax planning, compliance, and disputes with tax authorities. We offer guidance on minimizing tax liabilities, navigating complex tax regulations, and resolving tax disputes through negotiation or litigation.
